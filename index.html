Community driven & fair launch. The dev team burned all of their tokens and participated with everyone else. Every trade contributes towards automatically generating liquidity that goes into multiple pools used by exchanges. About Us

Baby Uni Whales employs 3 simple functions: Reflection + LP acquisition + Burn In each trade, the transaction is taxed a 10% fee, which is split 2 ways.
- 5% fee = redistributed to all existing holders
- 5% fee is split 50/50 half of which is sold by the contract into BNB, while the other half of the $BABYWHALES tokens are paired automatically with the previously mentioned BNB and added as a liquidity pair on Pancake Swap.
